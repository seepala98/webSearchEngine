Full course description

Document your C++ knowledge with the W3Schools C++ fundamentals exam to get the W3Schools certification. 

All questions in the exam are based on the content of W3Schools' C++ tutorial. We strongly recommend the following tutorial, quiz and exercises:

  • C++ Tutorial
  • C++ Quiz
  • C++ Exercises

The exam consists of 50 multiple choice or true/false questions. The exam is time-limited to 50 minutes.

Candidates must have 75% correct answers to pass the exam and become certified.

Immediately after completing the exam, you will be informed of your score and of your pass/fail status.

If you fail, or want to improve your score, you can take the exam one more time. The best result of the two attempts will count.
